Kisses are mandatory!

If you arent dusting your lips then you arent giving them enough kisses. Most pet Rocks demand kisses or else they retaliate with their own poo...
Best give them the kisses they want otherwise you will be cleaning rocky-road off your floor or carpet and it isn't going to be easy or fun.

Hymnrocks (Stage 3)

If you don't want to go insane listening to Barney songs all day then NO FOOD after midnight. It will inheritably altar its form into song form for 24 hours.
It is important to note that feeding it candy in this form will advance them to its monster form.

Grimrocks (Stage 3)

You are going to find yourself in a pickle if your pet Rock reaches its monster form as it likes to eat anything in sight. Luckily for you while it may seem intimidating, its bite may hurt but it wont draw blood. Note that it has a zipper which you can use to safely zip its mouth shut, however this is only temporary as it can undo its own zipper within 5 minutes. Rock monsters gain the ability to jump when its zipper remains open.
